## Work Bench ##

##### A mod adding a Work Bench for Minetest. #####
##### The Work Bench can cut up to 12 shapes, repair your tools, craft items and store. #####

##### This mod is originating from the X-Decor mod (https://github.com/kilbith/xdecor). #####
